About Me

My name is Zia Su, a product designer based in San Francisco Bay Area. I currently work at Groupon in the customer experience team. My passion for design centers on delivering delightful moments and improving the way people share information. I pursue the design solutions with finding the balance between empathy and business goal. Proficient in cross-platform design and prototyping.

Login Page
